Each level of a cookie takes an increasing amount of experience while at the same time being restricted by the level of your Kingdom Level.

The most efficient method of leveling up a Cookie is through feeding them Exp Star Jellies and the most reliable source of Exp Star Jellies is Cookie Houses although almost every feature and game mode also gives them.

Each Cookie House has its own level, production, and storage of Exp Jellies. They are not all combined to make one large storage so you need to regularly get on and collect the Exp Jellies that each Cookie House has collected.

The number of Cookie Houses and their maximum level is linked to the level of your Cookie Castle. For example at Cookie Castle level 15 each of your Cookie Houses can be upgraded to level 15 themselves and you can have up to 48 of them.

Once two Cookie Houses are upgraded to level 5 you can combine two equally leveled Cookie Houses to create a single Cookie Mansion. By combining them you double the production rate and candy capacity while reducing the space occupied by the Cookie Houses in half. The main benefit for combining however is that when you upgrade one Cookie Mansion it is the same as upgrading both Cookie Houses while only using one Sugar Gnome.

All candy produced by Cookie Houses and Cookie Mansions are level 1 candies so it is important that you have as many of them as you can build and to research Tastier Jelly to as high level as possible.

Tastier Jelly research increases the amount of experience that candies give by a certain percent depending on the level of the research.

Tastier Jelly research increases the amount of experience that candies give by a certain percent depending on the level of the research.

The research applies to all levels of candy but to each individual candy. This means that there no benefit to hoarding your candies and using them all at the same time for a bigger bonus. For example assuming you have Tastier Jelly research level 1, if you use 10,000 level 1 candies which give 14 each you will be getting 140,000 experience instead of 141,400 if the research bonus applied to the total instead of the individual.

The below formula is used to calculate the experience per candy with the Tastier Jelly research bonus as r, the base candy experience as c and rounded to the nearest whole number.

(c + (c * (r / 100))) => (14 + (14 * (1 / 100))) => 14.14 => 14
